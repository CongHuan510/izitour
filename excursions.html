Your Best Vietnam Tours Can Be Found Here

Amazing destinations expand from Northern to Southern tip of Vietnam. Be among multiethnic countries, Vietnam has over fifty four distinct groups, which have formed the cultural characteristics. Each destination is featured with its own history, natural beauty, unique culture and traditional gastronomy, which are intertwined to form a charming picture.

45 Unmissable Places For Your Best Tours in Vietnam

Vietnam’s top destinations are sorted into 7 groups according the similarities of typography and geography: Hanoi & Vicinity, Hue - Danang – Hoi An and vicinity,  Sapa and North West, Dalat and Central highland, Ha Giang& North East, Ho Chi Minh city & center, beaches. Taking a road trip from one destination to the others within a group or to a proximate group is so conveniently. Go specifically to each destination title to view its details.

Destination landscapes diversity            

Vietnam famous landscapes for natural mountainous scenery and eco-tourism includes: Mai Chau, NinhBinh, PuLuong, Sapa, Mu Cang Chai, Halong Bay, PhongNha, Dalat, Pleiku, Ha Giang, Cao Bang, etc … While Mai Chau’s lush green valley makes trekking the unforgettable experience, NinhBinh’s stunning countryside is best explored by bicycle and boat ride. Compared to Sapa, though PuLuong does not possess as many resorts and entertainment activities, it is authentic place to get back to nature.  There will be omission if not including Mu Cang Chai rice terraces, magnificent PhongNha caves, Halong Bay, Da Lat cloudy hill, breathtaking Ha Giang loops and many graceful waterfalls.

Travelling to further to rustic South Vietnam landscape, the people life and immense delta appear more calm and peaceful with range of coconut trees, floating market, floating fishing villages, scissor-crossed rivers, etc… Those destinations cover Ben Tre, Cai Be, Can Tho, Chau Doc, Long Xuyen, Sa Dec, Ho Chi Minh city’s outskirt, etc … Ben Tre reveals the confidential part of Mekong river downstream and connecting with Cai Be, Can Tho by waterway. In the water season, duckweed and water plant covering a vast area of Chau Doc make it valuable chance to observe.

Best Vietnam Tours focus on the the top destinations for cultural heritages are located mainly in central and southern region, from Hanoi to Hue, Hoi An, My Son sanctuary. With their long history and used to be the royal capital of feudal dynasty, Champa Kingdom, they still preserve the vestiges, monuments, old temples. Hanoi, Hue and Hoi An are likely living museums showing the prosperous period resulted from the vibrant trading work in the past. Slowly wandering among the relics and get to know how important these constructions are, how skillful the hands of worker were and what happened to them during the colonial period.

Heaven for Vietnam beach holidays

Travellers coming to Vietnam love spending few days lazing on Vietnam beaches, of which the most favored are Con Dao Island, NhaTrang, Mui Ne, PhuQuoc Island, QuyNhon and Vung Tau. Con Dao is a group of island captivating by its pristine remote beach, eponymous national park, as beautiful as PhuQuoc Island. If seeking for water activities like parasailing, paddling, jet skiing, water skiing, come to NhaTrang and Mui Ne. Burn energy and indulge yourselves under sunlight and into the fresh seawater.

Vietnamese cuisine

Somehow similar to the variety of the above Vietnam top destinations’ landscape, Vietnamese cuisine creates a map of tasty regional foods, changing from North to South. Thanks to this diversification, authentic Vietnamese cuisine can awaken your every taste bud more than one time. In general, the food is savory, however among regions there is the different appreciation for the yin and yang balance, the desirable presentation and street food. Joining in cooking class and roaming through street vendor will surprise eater with secret ingredients and recipes. 

Flexible accommodation

Hotels are available in almost destination, ranging from 3 stars to 5 stars in big city and less to select in some remote mountainous area. When visiting serene countryside, it is highly suggested to stay one or two nights at a homestay to sharing local experience with residents. A dream holiday will be fulfilled in style with some days at a seaside resorts at the end of the journey.
